# Momentum Board

Every stock in a broad US + UK universe, ranked by **12-1 momentum** and gated on **trend quality** (K-ratio smoothness, moving-average state, liquidity). An interactive single-page board: sort, filter, search, click through to Yahoo Finance.

**Live:** https://soylee22.github.io/momentum-board

## What it shows

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 12-1 mom | Total return over the 12 months ending one month ago (skip-month avoids short-term reversal) |
| K-ratio | slope ÷ standard error of a regression of log-price on time. Higher = smoother, steadier grind |
| Last-mo | Most recent month return. Watch a big negative here against a high 12-1: the trend may be breaking |
| vs 50/150/200 | Price relative to its moving averages. All green = textbook uptrend |
| S2 | Minervini Stage-2 trend template passed |
| ADV | Average daily $ traded (liquidity). Below ~$5m gets expensive to trade |
| 52wH | Distance from the 52-week high |

## Universe

S&P 500 + S&P 400 + S&P 600 + FTSE 100 + FTSE 250 + US $200m-$1bn names. Floor ~$200m, big caps included. The market-cap slider moves the floor live.

## Data

Generated by `dashboard_build.py` (yfinance prices, market-cap screener). A data-break guard drops denomination/stitch glitches (a >3x single-day move is a data error, not momentum) while keeping genuine monsters.

Snapshot data, survivorship-biased to current index members. Not investment advice.
